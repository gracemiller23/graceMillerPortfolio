Sampling of Modular Brochure Pages

GPD needed a brochure, but they often had to tailor it for a variety of B2B marketing contexts, from proposals to exploratory sessions. A single brochure seemed inefficient. To save them time and effort, I used Adobe InDesign, Photoshop, and Illustrator to create a modular file system of brochure pages targeted for different markets that they could pull from as needed, with only the need for minor adjustments.
