MARKETING COPY

Copywriting

They said I'd never use my rhetoric studies outside of academia, but they were wrong! Everything about writing has the power to persuade - from how you use punctuation to create flow to the tone you choose based on your audience. I love the challenge of creating copy that helps clients achieve their goals by effectively targeting their audiences. As a developer, I also have a unique perspective of digital readers and users that allows me to optimize web content even further.

MAGAZINE & WEBSITE COPY

Editorial Writing

While much of my career has been spent on the other side of the editorial line as an editor, I have been privileged to have my work published on occasion. For me, most writing is all about the readers. I enjoy the challenge of creating the tone and content that each outlet's readers will find relatable, informative, and enjoyable.

WHAT I WRITE FOR MYSELF

Creative Writing

Most of my writing is for readers ... but some of it is just for me. For as long as I can remember, creative writing has been free therapy. It's a place where I can make words dance to cadences that have no place in professional content - a place where the poetry and beauty of words can take flight. In all their forms, I believe in the deeply pervasive, and often under-appreciated power of words.
